Fujian Export Fair (Kuala Lumpur) 2016 Concurrently with Fujian Brand on Maritime Silk Road-Kuala Lumpur
China and Malaysia, two neighbours facing each other across the sea, enjoy a time-honoured friendship, with the trading record dated back to Common Era. With complimentary and mutual beneficial economic relationship, China has been Malaysia’s largest trading partner for seven years straight, and Malaysia is now China’s biggest trading partner among ASEAN countries. Among which Fujian and Malaysia’s trade volume has topped 4.34 billion dollars. China has set the goal to complete the building of a moderately prosperous society in all respects by 2020. It has much in common with Malaysia’s Vision 2020. As China is advancing the initiative of the Silk Road Economic Belt and the 21st Century Maritime Silk Road, encouraging mass entrepreneurship and innovation, and transforming and upgrading its economic structure, Malaysia is gearing toward all-round economic transformation
